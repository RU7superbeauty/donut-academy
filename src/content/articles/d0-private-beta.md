Today, we are introducing the D0 private beta: **a personal, secure, and intelligent trading agent, accessible via Telegram.** D0 transforms natural language intent into safe, fast, and auditable on-chain actions. This is not just another bot — it is the foundation of **agentic trading infrastructure**.

This journey began with a single belief: **every trader deserves a Day Zero** — a moment of clarity where the noise of a hundred fragmented tools disappears and only the trade remains.

## The Problem

The architecture of money has been rebuilt into a vast, programmable expanse. Yet for the individual trader, the experience remains **fractured**:

- **Tool Fragmentation** — A complete workflow is scattered across social feeds, block explorers, DEXs, and charting platforms.
- **Cognitive Overload** — In a 24/7 market, data volume overwhelms the ability to filter signal from noise.
- **The Execution Gap** — Decisions are made in calm moments but executed under flash-crash pressure.
- **The Time-for-Money Trap** — Your profit is limited by the physical hours you can stare at a screen.

## The DSEA Engine

The heart of D0 is the **DSEA loop** — Discovery, Strategy, Execution, and Attribution:

- **Discovery** — Continuously scans whale movements, liquidity shifts, social narratives, and macro signals.
- **Strategy** — You define the rules; the agent executes within your boundaries.
- **Execution** — Routes orders for the best achievable outcome across integrated venues.
- **Attribution** — Every action is documented and auditable.

## Security: Defense in Depth

- **Turnkey HSM** — Keys generated and stored inside Hardware Security Modules; never exposed.
- **Human-in-the-Loop** — All trades require explicit user confirmation.
- **Intent Verification** — AI's interpreted intent is displayed back before execution.
- **Position Limits** — Configurable max position size and leverage caps.
- **Slippage Protection** — Market orders include configurable slippage tolerance.
